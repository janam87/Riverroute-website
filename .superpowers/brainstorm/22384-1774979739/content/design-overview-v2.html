Visual Effects System

🎬 Hero Background Video
Cinematic production footage (film sets, camera rigs, behind-the-scenes). Plays muted, looped, with dark overlay so text stays readable.
🧊 Liquid Glass
Apple-style frosted glass on navbar, product cards, founder cards, and vertical grid items. Blur + subtle border + transparency.
📜 Parallax Scroll
Elements move at different speeds on scroll. Headlines drift slower, backgrounds shift. Creates depth. Framer Motion useScroll + useTransform.
✨ Section Reveal
Each section fades up from black as you scroll into it. Staggered children — headline first, then content, then details. Cinematic pacing.

Design Principles — Updated

Pure black. #000 or #0a0a0a background. No navy, no grey sections.
Bold serif. Playfair Display Black for all headlines. Inter for body.
No color accent. Black/white/grey only. Warmth from video and glass light refraction.
Liquid glass everywhere. Navbar, vertical grid, product cards, founder cards, input fields.
Cinematic video hero. Muted background video with dark overlay. Film/production footage.
Parallax depth. Headlines, stats, and cards at different scroll speeds. Creates cinematic layers.
Section reveals. Every section fades up from black. Staggered children. Nothing visible until you scroll to it.
Word-by-word text. Hero headline and key statements animate word-by-word.
Framer Motion. All animation via useScroll, useTransform, useInView, stagger.
Mobile-first. 7xl+ desktop, 4xl mobile. Glass effects degrade gracefully.
