Design Principles

Black, not navy. Pure #000 or near-black #0a0a0a background throughout.
White, not grey. Primary text is #fff. Secondary is #444–#666. Nothing in between.
Serif for display. Playfair Display Black for all headlines. Inter for body.
No color. No gold, no accent. Warmth comes from photography and motion, not palette.
Cinematic motion. Word-by-word reveals, parallax, staggered entries. Framer Motion throughout.
Breathe. Massive whitespace (blackspace). Let each statement land before the next one starts.
Mobile-first. Text scales dramatically — 7xl+ on desktop, 4xl on mobile.
